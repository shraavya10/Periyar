About the Author

The Author,P Shraavya Hande, is a student at EKYA School, J P Nagar,Bengaluru.She is 13 yrs old.She has an immense interest in reading biographies of social reformers and so was afflicted to writing this book.The language used by the author is simple and caters to all group of readers. This is her first venture as a writer of biography.Her earlier work was a school book on "Conventional and non conventional resources".Her main audience has been school kids.
